About this Design System

This Design System defines the rules, components, and rationale behind every visual and interaction decision for Roots & Routes. It is structured across seven dedicated pages, mirroring how design systems are maintained in professional practice.

Every decision links back to a research finding (F1–F5) or design requirement (DR1–DR6). The system is designed to make A3 implementation straightforward and consistent.
